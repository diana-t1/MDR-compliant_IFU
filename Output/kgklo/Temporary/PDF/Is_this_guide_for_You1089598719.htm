Is this a guide for You?

Creating Instructions for Use (IFU) that comply with Medical Device Regulation (MDR) is not a one-person task—it takes a team of experts, each playing a crucial role in ensuring clarity, accuracy, and compliance. From manufacturers shaping the initial content to regulatory specialists refining legal aspects, many professionals contribute to making IFU documents accessible and effective. Technical writers ensure that instructions are user-friendly, while healthcare professionals and clinical experts provide real-world insights that enhance safety and usability. Together, they build a document that not only meets regulations but also helps users confidently operate medical devices.

Are You one of the following? Role in IFU Development
Medical device manufacturer Ensure IFU meet MDR requirements and provide clear usage instructions.
Regulatory affairs specialist Interpret MDR guidelines and ensure compliance with legal regulations.
Technical writer Create user-friendly and well-structured IFU documents that meet accessibility and language standards.
Healthcare professional Review IFU content to ensure accuracy in real-world medical settings.
Clinical expert Provide insights into patient safety and usability considerations.
