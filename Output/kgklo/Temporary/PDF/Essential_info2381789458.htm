Essential information to include

A well-structured MDR-compliant IFU must provide clear, concise, and legally required details to ensure safe and effective device usage. Essential information to include are:

  • Intended use of the device – Provide information of how, by who, where and in what conditions the device can be used.

  • Warnings & precautions – Highlight risks, contraindications, and safety measures to prevent misuse. See more about "Clear warnings and precautions" on page 1

  • Step-by-Step usage instructions – Provide clear guidance on how to operate the device correctly, supplemented with diagrams where necessary. See more about "Step-by-Step instructions" on page 1

  • Maintenance & cleaning – Outline proper care, sterilization, and reprocessing instructions, especially for reusable devices. Learn more with "Maintenance, cleaning, and disposal guidelines" on page 1

  • Disposal guidelines – Specify how to safely discard the device and its components, including hazardous materials.

  • Performance specifications – Detail the device's capabilities, ensuring users understand its limitations.

Depending on the specifics of the device, there may be precise requirements as to what information must be provided in the IFU according to the MDR. See "Key MDR compliance criteria" on page 1 for more information.
