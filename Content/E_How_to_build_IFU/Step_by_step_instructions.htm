Step-by-Step instructions

For complex medical devices, such as surgical instruments, implantable devices, diagnostic equipment, and reusable tools, clear step-by-step instructions are crucial to ensure safe and effective use. Under Medical Device Regulation (MDR 2017/745), IFU must outline each procedural step in a way that is structured, concise, and easy to follow.

For devices that require assembly, calibration, or sterilization, IFU should emphasize safety precautions, detailing potential errors or risks users must avoid.

Formatting tips:

  • Use numbered lists to guide users through actions in the correct order.

  • Keep instructions short and precise — avoid unnecessary detail.

  • Include visual aids, such as diagrams or icons, to illustrate key steps.

  • Highlight critical actions (e.g., safety checks, calibration, sterilization) using bold text or callout boxes.

  • Ensure logical sequencing, from device preparation to usage and post-use maintenance.
