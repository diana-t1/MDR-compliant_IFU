Clear Instructions for Use (IFU) are key to ensuring medical devices are used safely and correctly. With the Medical Device Regulation (MDR) replacing the Medical Device Directive (MDD), the focus has shifted toward better usability, transparency, and patient safety.

This guide covers the essentials of MDR-compliant IFU – what to include, how to structure them, and how to make them as clear and accessible as possible. Let’s simplify compliance while keeping instructions practical and effective!
